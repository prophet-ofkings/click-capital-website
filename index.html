Platform Questions

How does Click Capital work?

Click Capital is a digital platform that enables fractional ownership of premium assets including real estate and rare commodities. Simply create an account, complete your KYC verification for security compliance, browse our curated collection of properties and commodities, select opportunities that interest you starting from as little as AED 2,500, and track everything through your personal dashboard. All transactions are processed digitally with complete transparency, giving you 24/7 access to manage your portfolio from anywhere in the world.

What makes Click Capital different?

Click Capital revolutionizes access to premium assets through several key differentiators: We offer fractional ownership starting from just AED 2,500, making luxury real estate and rare commodities accessible to everyone. Our platform provides 24/7 digital access with instant portfolio tracking. We feature diversified opportunities across real estate, gold, diamonds, and rubies. Every asset undergoes rigorous verification with complete documentation transparency. Our secondary marketplace provides liquidity options unavailable in traditional ownership. All properties are professionally managed by expert teams, and our technology stack ensures bank-grade security with real-time updates.

When does the platform launch?

Click Capital is scheduled to launch in Q1 2026. Join our waitlist today to secure exclusive early access before the public launch. Waitlist members will receive priority access to our inaugural property offerings, advance platform tutorials, and founder member benefits. We'll keep you updated throughout our development process and notify you immediately when the platform goes live.

What is fractional ownership?

Fractional ownership is a modern approach that allows multiple participants to collectively own shares of a single high-value asset. Each participant owns a specific percentage of the property or commodity, represented by secure digital tokens on our platform. This innovative model enables you to access premium properties worth millions of dollars or rare commodities with a fraction of the capital traditionally required. You receive proportional benefits including rental income distributions from properties and participation in potential value appreciation. It's like owning a piece of a luxury property or precious commodity without needing to purchase it entirely yourself.

How are benefits distributed?

For real estate opportunities, rental income is collected from tenants by professional property management teams and distributed to owners on a quarterly basis, proportional to your ownership percentage. For commodities like gold, diamonds, and rubies, value changes are reflected in real-time on your dashboard based on market conditions. All distributions are processed automatically through the platform with complete transparency. You can view detailed reporting showing exactly where distributions come from, when they were processed, and your proportional share. The entire process is digital, eliminating delays associated with traditional real estate ownership.

What is the minimum participation amount?

Click Capital makes premium opportunities accessible with a minimum participation starting at $2,500 USD for most offerings. This entry point allows you to start building a diversified portfolio of luxury real estate and rare commodities. Different properties and commodities may have varying minimums based on their total value and ownership structure, but we're committed to maintaining accessible entry points. This democratized access allows participants to own fractions of properties and assets that would typically require hundreds of thousands or millions of dollars to purchase outright.

How secure is the platform?

Security is our highest priority at every level. All real estate properties undergo comprehensive due diligence including legal verification, title searches, and market analysis before listing. Physical commodities (gold, diamonds, rubies) are certified by international standards (GIA for diamonds, LBMA for gold) and stored in secure, insured vaults in the UAE. Our digital infrastructure uses bank-grade encryption to protect your data and transactions. We implement multi-factor authentication, secure wallet technology, and regular security audits. Additionally, we're working closely with regulatory bodies in the UAE and KSA to ensure full compliance with all financial regulations, providing you with peace of mind that your ownership is legitimate and protected.

Can I sell my fractional ownership?

Yes! One of Click Capital's key advantages is our integrated secondary marketplace. Unlike traditional real estate which can take months to sell, you can list your fractional ownership tokens on our marketplace where other verified platform users can purchase them. This provides significantly more liquidity and flexibility compared to conventional property ownership. The marketplace operates 24/7, allowing you to list your holdings whenever you choose. While liquidity depends on market demand and interest in specific properties or commodities, this system eliminates the lengthy processes, agent commissions, and paperwork typically associated with selling real estate or physical commodities.

How are properties selected and managed?

Our expert team conducts rigorous due diligence on every potential property opportunity. We evaluate multiple factors including prime location desirability, current market trends and growth projections, rental demand and income potential, property condition and maintenance requirements, legal title clarity and documentation, and long-term appreciation prospects. Only properties that meet our strict criteria are listed on the platform. Once acquired, properties are managed by professional property management companies with proven track records. These teams handle all aspects including tenant screening and relations, regular maintenance and repairs, rent collection and financial reporting, legal compliance, and emergency response. You receive detailed monthly reports showing property performance, occupancy rates, maintenance activities, and financial distributions.

What about commodity authentication?

All commodities on Click Capital undergo strict authentication and certification processes. Gold must meet LBMA (London Bullion Market Association) standards for purity and be 99.99% pure. Diamonds are certified by GIA (Gemological Institute of America) with detailed reports on cut, clarity, color, and carat weight. Rubies are authenticated by certified gemologists and come with complete provenance documentation. All physical commodities are stored in secure, climate-controlled vaults in the UAE with comprehensive insurance coverage. You can view certification documents and storage details for each commodity directly through your dashboard, ensuring complete transparency and peace of mind about the authenticity and security of your holdings.

Is Click Capital regulated?

Click Capital is registered and operates from Sharjah Innovation and Technology Park in the United Arab Emirates. We are actively working with regulatory authorities including the Dubai Financial Innovation Centre (DFIC) in the UAE and the Capital Market Authority (CMA) in Saudi Arabia. Our applications for regulatory approval are currently pending, and we are committed to full compliance with all applicable regulations. We work closely with legal advisors to ensure our platform meets the highest standards of regulatory compliance, consumer protection, and financial transparency. The platform will only launch for public access after receiving appropriate regulatory approvals, ensuring you participate through a fully compliant and regulated platform.

How liquid are my holdings?

Traditional real estate ownership can take months or even years to liquidate, involving lengthy negotiations, paperwork, and high transaction costs. Click Capital transforms this through our secondary marketplace. You can list your fractional ownership tokens at any time, and other platform users can purchase them, typically completing transactions within days rather than months. Liquidity varies based on factors like property desirability, current market conditions, and overall platform demand. Popular properties in prime locations generally attract more buyer interest. The marketplace operates continuously, giving you flexibility to exit positions when needed. This represents a fundamental advantage over traditional real estate and commodity ownership, where selling can be complex and time-consuming.

Who can use Click Capital?

Click Capital welcomes participants from most countries worldwide, subject to local regulations and compliance requirements. During registration, all users complete a KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process to comply with international anti-money laundering standards and financial regulations. This typically involves providing identification documents, proof of address, and basic background information. The process is entirely digital and usually completes within 24-48 hours. Some jurisdictions may have specific restrictions or additional requirements, which we'll clearly communicate during onboarding. Our goal is to make premium asset ownership accessible globally while maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance and user security. If you're unsure about your eligibility, our support team can provide guidance based on your location.

Important Notice

Fractional ownership of real estate and commodities involves significant risks. You may lose some or all of your participation amount. This disclosure outlines key risks, but may not cover all potential risks. This disclosure is subject to change without notice.

1. Market Risk

Property values and commodity prices fluctuate based on market conditions, economic factors, supply and demand, and other variables beyond our control. Past performance does not indicate future results. Market downturns can result in decreased values and potential losses.

2. Liquidity Risk

While Click Capital provides a secondary marketplace, there is no guarantee you will be able to sell your fractional ownership when desired or at your preferred price. Market demand varies, and you may need to accept lower prices or wait extended periods to complete a sale.

3. Property-Specific Risks

Real estate opportunities carry risks including:

  • Vacancy periods resulting in reduced or no rental income
  • Property damage requiring significant repair costs
  • Tenant defaults or legal disputes
  • Unexpected maintenance or capital expenditure needs
  • Changes in local real estate markets or regulations
  • Natural disasters, environmental issues, or force majeure events

4. Commodity Risks

Physical commodities (gold, diamonds, rubies) involve risks including:

  • Price volatility based on global market conditions
  • Currency exchange rate fluctuations
  • Storage and insurance costs
  • Changes in commodity demand and market sentiment
  • Authenticity concerns despite certification processes

5. Platform Risk

Risks related to the Click Capital platform include:

  • Technology failures, outages, or cybersecurity breaches
  • Changes to platform features, fees, or operations without notice
  • Platform suspension or discontinuation
  • Regulatory changes affecting platform operations
  • Third-party service provider failures

6. Regulatory Risk

Click Capital is pending regulatory approval. Changes in regulations could affect platform operations, available opportunities, or your ability to participate. Regulatory developments are unpredictable and subject to change without notice.

7. No Guaranteed Returns

There are no guaranteed returns, income, or profits. Projected yields, rental income estimates, and value appreciation projections are estimates only and may not materialize. Actual results may differ significantly from projections.

8. Concentration Risk

Limiting participation to few properties or commodity types increases exposure to specific market risks. Diversification does not eliminate risk but may help manage exposure.

9. Tax Implications

Fractional ownership may have tax consequences varying by jurisdiction. You are responsible for understanding and complying with all tax obligations. Click Capital does not provide tax advice.

10. No Insurance or Guarantees

Your fractional ownership is not insured by any government agency or guarantee program. While properties and commodities may be insured against specific risks, this does not protect against market value losses.

11. Changes to Risk Disclosures

This risk disclosure is subject to change at any time without notice. Additional risks may emerge, and disclosed risks may change in magnitude or nature. You are responsible for staying informed about risks.

12. Acknowledgment

By using Click Capital, you acknowledge understanding these risks and accept full responsibility for your participation decisions. You should only participate with amounts you can afford to lose.
